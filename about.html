Our Style Our Youth

In today’s modern world, technology is everywhere; youths have their own gadgets that they use on a daily basis. This young generation enjoys attending different kinds of events to socialize. Junior and Senior years range from 12- 18 years old. This is the time where young people hit puberty. Fashion is used by young people as a way of self-expression, building social bonds with their peers, and expressing their affinities. Adolescence is characterized by the desire for a unique identity, and clothing choices have a significant contribution to the development of that identity. The Second Year College students of the Bachelor of Science in Information System at the Technological University of the Philippines developed a plan to create a website called "Confianza & Vogue".

The Confianza & Vogue website is a fashion visual discovery engine that proposes where to buy, what brands are affordable for teenagers, and what outfits match the event to which they are going. This website functions as a clothing library, recommending various outfits based on different factors like body type, climate, budget and many more. It is a portmanteau of the words "confidence," "fashion," and "trends." It includes a variety of categories from which the youth will be gladly inspired to select ahead of time and confidently style an outfit. Furthermore, this website will provide the target audience with an understanding of the fashion world, allowing them to appreciate it and stay updated with current trends.

BENEFITS

Youths

This website will help the junior and senior high school students to explore and learn about their personal style. Thus, it will serve as their guide in everything that concerns fashion.

World

In today’s time, the fact that the fashion industry contributes significantly to global pollution is undeniable. This website will therefore introduce sustainable fashion to its target users. It is crucial that these young people value clothing and dress responsibly because they will constitute the majority of the next generation.

Local Stores

One of the features of the website Confianza & Vogue will highlight some of the best local stores that are budget friendly since the target users are Junior and Senior high school students.
